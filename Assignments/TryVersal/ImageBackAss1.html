The Dolomiti, or Dolomites, in northern Italy are a breathtaking masterpiece of nature, where jagged peaks rise dramatically against the sky. Renowned for their striking limestone formations, these mountains glow in hues of pink and gold during sunrise and sunset, a phenomenon known as alpenglow.

The Dolomites in northern Italy are a breathtaking mountain range known for their dramatic peaks, rugged cliffs, and stunning alpine scenery. A UNESCO World Heritage Site, the Dolomites offer a paradise for outdoor enthusiasts, with world-class hiking, skiing, and climbing opportunities.
